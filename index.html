Dave Mirra

The Beginning

Dave grew up in a small town called Chittenango, NY. As a burbs boy he didn't have shit to do so he had to make his own fun. Dude picked up a bike and rode it around with his friends. "Hell yea this shits sick!" he thought.Then one of his homies rode a bike off a curb. "Ohhhh hell nah" Dave said, and he busted a fat j-hop off a curb cut like a G. His dudes were impressed, and bought him a pound of maple bacon to congradulate him.

Haro Bikes

Mirra quickly became the talk of the town. Old people would say "That boy is gonna hurt himself very badly, so badly he may even need to see a doctor or something." That is a direct quote. After a while of stealing everyones girls, Haro bikes took notice. They knew this guy had something special, and put him on the team at the ripe age of 13 in 1987. Dave now not only wanted to be the best in the town he wanted to be the best to ever do the damn thing.

The Man, The Myth, The Mirra

Awwwwwwww shit it was on! Dave was going hard as hell learning all the stunts and creating new ones that made people freak tf out. He met Nyquist and they started to push eachother to levels BMX had never seen. Dave went pro in 1992, and was ready to show the world what he got. X-Games BMX had never seen such a powerhouse. He won a medal at every X-Games from 1995-2009 with the exception of 2006 cus he felt like givin some new guys a shot, but then he came back like fuck yall I'm still the greatest.

Video Game

Lezbereal: one of Mirra's greatest accomplishments was scoring a fuckin video game. Everyone had that shit, which ended up exposing BMX to such a wide variety of antisocial teens. Honestly Dave is probably responsible for introducing BMX to more people than any other single human in history.

Other Stuff

Dave was a real 21st century rennaisance man. He went on to race little cars through dirt jumps, ride road bikes at 200mph across oceans, and raise a wonderful family.

Death

Unfortunately being an athlete on the level of Dave Mirra takes its tolls. He suffered many injuries and the one that did him in was repeated head trauma. CTE is no joke, protect your head people. Dave committed suicide for reasons unknown.
